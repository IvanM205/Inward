# 01 — Product Overview

## Mission

Free the user's mind from systems of extraction (feeds, shorts, binge series, games,
betting, pornography, substances, engineered nightlife, materialism, outsourced
thinking / AI-dependence, spectator life) and return their attention to chosen values:
love, family, compassion, friendship, home, nature, kindness, empathy.

Inward is a **compass, not a casino**: a daily practice of reorientation, away from
power/money/stimulation, toward love/depth/presence. Cross-traditional, dogma-free,
welcoming to believers and atheists alike.

## The four movements (product arc)

| # | Movement | Room(s) | What it does |
|---|---|---|---|
| I | **SEE — The Mirror** | Mirror | Intake conversation/quiz → Extraction Index across 12 channels → merciful portrait → conscious knowing |
| II | **LOOSEN — The Untangling** | Plan | Liberation Plan (one channel per season), habit replacement, Craving Button, dare ladder, Build One Thing |
| III | **AWAKEN — The Ascent** | Library, Companion | One Deep Thing daily reading, Paths of Awakening, four practices (silence, gratitude, awe, service), Companion counsel |
| IV | **LIVE — The Daily Return** | Threshold, Quiet | Morning/evening Compass, Today's Opening, Living Journal, the Needle, weekly Realignment, Unplug/Detox/Stillness |

## Information architecture

Single home screen (**The Threshold**) showing only today's state (≤ 3 elements).
Doors to: **Mirror**, **Plan**, **Library**, **Companion**, plus the **Quiet** switch.
Navigation rule: no screen can reach more content of the same kind; every path
terminates (in the world, in silence, or in the closed app). No loops, only exits.

## Target users

Adults who feel captured by the attention economy and want out. The product assumes
no religious affiliation, no prior meditation experience, near-zero starting
willpower (first asked action: one breath).

## Glossary

- **Threshold** — home screen; shows only what today asks.
- **Extraction Index** — per-channel 0–100 capture score; see `04-scoring-spec.md`.
- **Bands** — Free (0–25), Leaking (26–60), Caught (61–100).
- **Portrait** — single-page result of the Mirror.
- **Thread** — one channel under active loosening for a season (6–12 weeks).
- **Season** — 6–12 week period; exactly one active thread.
- **Dare ladder** — 7 escalating real-world challenges per thread.
- **Living Journal / Evidence Journal** — private journal; entry types: `kindness`,
  `care`, `aliveness`, plus auto entries (`dare_done`, `craving_decoded`,
  `path_reflection`, `gratitude`).
- **The Needle** — 90-day drift visualization of evening reflections (spirit ↔ matter).
- **Today's Opening** — the single daily act offered from the active thread.
- **Realignment** — weekly 10-minute review (Ledger + values + Circle + one commitment).
- **The Quiet** — Unplug Mode (1–4 h), Dopamine Detox (7/14/30 d), Stillness Switch
  (weekly recurring protected rest).
- **Open Hand** — pay-what-you-want patronage (€3/€8/€15/custom), monthly ask.
- **Circle** — group of 5–8 people sharing one weekly reflection and one shared dare.
- **Reverse retention** — the only success metric (see README).
